How it works

Three ways to earn

Every healthy habit earns you $CPD tokens. The more you move, think, and learn — the more you earn.

🏃

Move to Earn

Walk, run, and work out. Your steps are tracked via GPS and motion sensors. Compete in global step challenges with runners from 50+ countries.

Up to +24 $CPD per day

🧠

Think to Earn

Daily brain training, mindfulness logs, cognitive challenges and focus sessions. Mental health is as important as physical health in CUPID.

Up to +16 $CPD per day

📚

Study to Earn

Complete learning modules, share knowledge globally, mentor others. Education is the foundation of a better world — and it pays in CUPID.

Up to +8 $CPD per day

Our Philosophy

Inspired by The Venus Project

"The purpose of the Venus Project is to help all people regardless of race, religion, or nationality realize their common heritage and improve their lives." — Jacque Fresco, The Venus Project

CUPID extends this vision into the digital age. We believe healthy people build a better world — and that technology should reward good habits, not exploit them.
